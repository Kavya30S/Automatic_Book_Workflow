"The Gates of Morning" is a novel by Henry De Vere Stacpoole. The book is about a boy who travels through the world on a canoe. The story is told in the form of a series of short chapters. The first chapter is titled "The Canoe Builder" The second chapter is called "The Sea of Karolin" The third and final chapter is known as "The Gate of the Morning" The book was written in the first half of the 20th century and is published by Penguin Books.
